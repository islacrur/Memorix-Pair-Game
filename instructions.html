HOW TO PLAY

Welcome to the circus ring!
Upon opening, you will see all the cards face down in a grid.
Matching Cards:
Tap/click on a card to pick it up. If the cards match, you've found a match! The cards will stay up. If they are not the same, the cards will return to their face down position. Continue clicking on the rest of the cards until you find all the pairs. Each two taps/click counts as one attempt.
Gain:
The game ends when all the pairs have been found. Try to make as few attempts as possible.
Good luck!
